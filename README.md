# Marketing Performance Analysis

## Project Description
This project analyzes marketing performance to evaluate the efficiency of customer acquisition channels and optimize budget allocation.

---

## Objective
Assess marketing efficiency using key metrics such as CAC, LTV, and ROMI to improve investment decisions.

---

## Dataset
- Website visits  
- Orders and revenue  
- Marketing costs by channel  

---

## Tools Used
- Python (Pandas)  
- Matplotlib / Seaborn  
- Cohort analysis  

---

## Analysis Process

- User behavior analysis  
- Conversion analysis  
- CAC and LTV calculation  
- ROMI evaluation  
- Cohort analysis  

---

## Key Insights

- The overall ROMI is negative (-23.42%), indicating inefficient budget allocation.  
- Channels 1 and 2 show the best performance.  
- Channel 3 generates the highest losses (~68%).  
- Channel 9 presents a strong growth opportunity.  

---

## Recommendations

- Reallocate budget toward high-performing channels  
- Reduce investment in low-performing channels  
- Optimize high-traffic but low-conversion channels  
- Continuously monitor key metrics  

---

## Expected Impact

- ROMI improvement from -23.42% to 35–40%  
- Reduction of losses by ~$95,000  
- Increase in revenue by ~$60,000  

---

## Conclusion

Efficient budget allocation based on data-driven insights can significantly improve marketing performance and profitability.

---

## Business Impact

- Identified inefficient marketing spend with a negative ROMI of -23%  
- Proposed budget reallocation strategy to improve ROMI up to 40%  
- Estimated reduction in losses of ~$95,000 annually
  
---
## Next Steps

- Implement continuous monitoring  
- Perform A/B testing on campaigns
